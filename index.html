About us

Rooted in science, driven by compassion

Farmavet Services was founded with a clear mission: to provide reliable, professional, and compassionate veterinary care for both farm animals and pets.

Led by Dr Joseph Abou Nasser, a Doctor of Veterinary Medicine specialized in production animals, our clinic brings together years of field experience — including work with major dairy farms, international organizations like the FAO, and advanced training in France. We understand the challenges farmers face and the importance of preventive care, productivity, and rapid response when it comes to herd health.

At the same time, we know how much your pets mean to you. That's why we also offer trusted pet care services, from vaccinations and medical treatment to proper nutrition and hygiene.

What sets us apart

  • Expertise in large animal medicine and reproductive services
  • A fully equipped veterinary pharmacy with quality medicines and pet supplies
  • Personalized attention for both farm animals and companion animals
  • Commitment to animal welfare, biosecurity, and client satisfaction

Our products

Trusted brands for livestock & pets

A wide range of high-quality veterinary products to ensure the health, productivity, and well-being of your animals — with expert guidance.

Livestock Products

  • Veterinary medicines (antibiotics, anti-inflammatories, reproductive hormones)
  • Vaccines (clostridial, respiratory, enterotoxemia, etc.)
  • Antiparasitics (injectable, oral, pour-on)
  • Minerals and vitamin supplements
  • Mastitis treatments and udder care products
  • AI and reproductive tools (syringes, gloves, PGF2α, GnRH, etc.)
  • Calf tonics and oral rehydration salts

Pet Products

  • Vaccines and preventive care (anti-flea, tick, and deworming)
  • Prescription medications for common illnesses
  • Nutritional supplements for joints, coat, and digestion
  • Josera® premium pet food (puppy, adult, senior, special diets)
  • Shampoos and hygiene products
  • Basic accessories (bowls, leashes, collars, brushes)

All products are available at our licensed veterinary pharmacy, with veterinary advice on proper use and dosing.
